About
Hi there! 👋
I am Ivan Moriá Borges, a Brazilian Music Therapist with a Master's in Neurosciences, and a PhD Candidate currently serving as a Professor at UFMG (2025–2027).
As a multi-instrumentalist and researcher, I work at the intersection of Music Therapy, Neurosciences, and Computational Music Analysis. My work focus on Music Information Retrieval (MIR) and the development of digital tools for micro-analysis and clinical assessment in music therapy.
